Yudong Guo

I got my Ph.D. degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2021, supervised by Prof. Juyong Zhang. Before that, I received bachelor degree in Statistics in 2015 from USTC. From fall 2016 to spring 2017, I was a research assistant in the MultiMedia Lab at Nanyang Technological University under supervision of Prof. Jianfei Cai and Prof. Jianmin Zheng. My research interests include 3D vision and digital human.
